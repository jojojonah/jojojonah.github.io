Most, if not all of these works were made when I was between the ages of 16, 17 and 18, and are not a current nor visual representation of the way I feel as a person at the age of 24. However, they still were once a huge part of me and I will always and forever embrace that. I want to send a huge shoutout and thank you to any of those who helped me create them. A big regret of mine is not including more people of color and more specifically *black people* into the creation of my works at the time and that will always be a hindurance and an impediment of mine when I look back but aye it is what it is and I can't go back. I've grown and matured a lot more now and i'm more educated on certain matters than I was at the age of 16 or 17, but anyway, I hope you all enjoyed what you saw and were stimulated enough to want to return again in the hopes of new works being created.
